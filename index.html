Short Bio

Yao Ma is an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
He got his PhD from Michigan State University in 2021 under the supervision of Dr. Jiliang Tang. Before that, he completed his MS (2016) in Statistics, Probability & Operations Research at Eindhoven University of Technology and BS (2015) in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Zhejiang University.

I have several fully-funded PhD positions available in Spring 2024, and Fall 2024. If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to email me. Please check here for more details.

Research Interests
